What You Get

Everything in one search

Official HUD Designations

Every search returns live HUD Qualified Census Tract status, CDBG Low-to-Moderate Income eligibility, and Opportunity Zone 2.0 eligibility — pulled directly from official HUD and ACS source data. No manual lookup required.

Live Grant Deadlines

Matched grant programs are checked against the Grants.gov live feed so you see current application deadlines alongside each result — not stale program descriptions. Formula grants are flagged separately so you know exactly what requires an application.

Federal Funding History

How It Works

Three steps, one address

1

Enter any address

TractPitch geocodes the address via the Census Bureau geocoder and identifies the exact census tract using PostGIS spatial lookup.

2

Demographics are pulled

The tract's 2022 ACS demographic profile — income, poverty rate, minority share, renter rate, unemployment — is retrieved and screened against 20+ program eligibility thresholds.

3

Full results returned

Every matched grant is returned with qualifying reasons, live deadlines, HUD designation badges, and three years of federal funding history. Download as a formatted PDF report.
